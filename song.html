Carol of the Bells

Carol of the Bells is an adaptation of an ancient Ukrainian folk songcalled a "shchedrivka". An arrangement by Mykola Leontovych (1877-1921)was popularized in the 1930's by Oleksander Koshyts (1875-1944), aUkrainian choir director who worked in the US and Canada. It has sincebecome an American Christmas classic.
